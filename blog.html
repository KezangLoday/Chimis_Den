A Serene Visit to Buddha Point

Embarked on a peaceful journey to Buddha Point, a haven of tranquility amidst the bustling city. The giant Buddha statue is not only awe-inspiring but the panoramic views of Thimphu valley from the point are breathtaking. August 22, 2022

Trek to Taktsang Monastery

The trek to the iconic Taktsang Monastery was nothing short of magical. Perched on a cliff, the monastery is a testament to Bhutan's architectural genius and spiritual sanctity. The trek through beautiful pine forests was as enriching as the destination itself. A blend of adventure and spirituality that's etched in my heart.

A Visit to the Picturesque Paro

Paro welcomed us with its quaint charm and rich history. A walk through the ancient streets, visits to the Rinpung Dzong and the National Museum, offered a glimpse into Bhutan's rich cultural tapestry. The blend of natural beauty and historical significance made this visit an unforgettable one.

October 21, 2021

Celebrating Thimphu Tshechu

Experiencing Thimphu Tshechu was like stepping into a vibrant world of traditions. The colorful mask dances and cultural performances were spellbinding, showcasing Bhutan's deeply rooted Buddhist traditions. The communal festivity and spiritual fervor were palpable, making it a celebration to remember.

Hike to Phajoding

The hike to Phajoding was a blend of physical challenge and spiritual rejuvenation. The trail through dense forests opened up to reveal the majestic monastery and pristine high altitude lakes. The tranquility of Phajoding is unmatched, making it a perfect escape for nature and spirituality enthusiasts alike.
